Bullet with proper indent (2 spaces = marker + 1):

- item
  continued

Bullet with insufficient indent (1 space - NOT part of item):

- item
 not continued

Ordered list multi-digit marker (10. = 3 chars, need 4 indent):

10. item
    continued

Ordered list insufficient indent (3 spaces - NOT part of item):

10. item
   not continued

Simple ordered marker (1. = 2 chars, need 3 indent):

1. item
   continued

Simple ordered insufficient (2 spaces - NOT part of item):

1. item
  not continued

Multiple spaces after marker (align to first non-space):

-  item with extra space
   continued at 3 spaces

Plus marker:

+ item
  continued

Star marker:

* item
  continued

Nested list continuation:

- outer
  - inner
    inner continued
  outer continued

Mixed paragraphs and lists:

Some text before.

- list item
  with continuation

Some text after.
