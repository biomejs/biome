Tight Lists

A tight list has no blank lines between items:

  • Item 1
  • Item 2
  • Item 3

Ordered tight list:

  1. First
  2. Second
  3. Third

Loose Lists

A loose list has blank lines between items:

  • Loose item 1

  • Loose item 2

  • Loose item 3

Ordered loose list:

  1. First loose

  2. Second loose

  3. Third loose

Mixed - Tight with Multi-line Content

Tight list where items have multiple lines (still tight):

  • Item one continues here
  • Item two also continues

Nested Lists - Mixed Tightness

Outer tight, inner loose:

  • Outer 1

    • Inner 1

    • Inner 2

  • Outer 2

Outer loose, inner tight:

  • Outer A

  • Outer B

    • Inner A
    • Inner B
